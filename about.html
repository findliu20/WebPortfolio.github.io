You will be surprised that I have completed this very website when only 4 weeks ago I knew nothing about coding. Up until now, I've always thought that my job was to design, design and design. No matter how deep I get into the field, it's never going to be about coding, and there's no reason why a designer needs to know about code.

It's like you know too little to contribute in this field of web development, so is the little knowledge you can learn as a designer useful? The fact that a designer knows how to code in a basic way, can be a way to better understand the coder's job, so that the products he designs are not too out of reach and what obstacles will it have to it becomes more practical.

I must say a big thank to my teacher: Mrs Shruthi. She certainly broke down a barrier in my knowledge and preconceptions about coding, that I used to think the code was boring, difficult, and had nothing to do with design. That was a mistake.
